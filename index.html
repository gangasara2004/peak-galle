◈ Destinations

Trip Highlights

01
🏰
Galle Fort

Walk through 400 years of history inside the iconic Dutch colonial fortification. UNESCO World Heritage Site with stunning ramparts overlooking the Indian Ocean.

02
🚨
Galle lighthouse

Stand beneath the oldest lighthouse in Sri Lanka. Built in 1848, it's one of the most photographed landmarks on the island with breathtaking ocean views.

03
🏖️
Unawatuna beach

One of Sri Lanka's most famous beaches. Crystal clear turquoise waters, golden sand, and swaying palms make this the perfect tropical escape.

04
🌿
Jungle Beach

A hidden gem tucked behind lush jungle. Accessible only by foot through a forest trail, this secluded cove offers pristine waters and raw natural beauty.

◈ Information

Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the trip?+

Transport by non-AC bus, visit to Galle Fort, lighthouse, Unawatuna Beach, Jungle Beach, and basic trip guidance. Meals and personal expenses are not included unless mentioned.

What should I wear and bring?+

Wear light and comfortable clothes, walking shoes, a hat, sunscreen, a water bottle, and your National ID or school ID. A small bag for beach essentials is also helpful.

How does the payment and booking work?+

Fill out the registration form, transfer the full ticket amount, and upload your payment slip. Once your payment is approved, you will receive confirmation details.

Is the trip suitable for all age groups?+

Yes, the trip is suitable for most age groups. Since there is some walking and beach time, comfortable footwear and caution near the coast are recommended.

What is the cancellation policy?+

Cancellation terms will be shared by the organizers. Please contact the team as early as possible if you need to change your plan.

Who is organizing this trip?+

This Galle day trip is organized by PEAK Adventures, with a focus on safe, well-guided coastal exploration and student-friendly travel experiences.
