◈ Information

Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the trip?+

Transport via AC coach, guided tours at all destinations, and entrance fees. Meals are at your own expense unless specified.

What should I wear and bring?+

Warm clothing (10–15°C mornings), comfortable walking shoes, a light rain jacket, sunscreen, and your National ID or passport.

How does the payment and booking work?+

Submit the booking form, then make your payment via bank transfer and upload your payment slip. Once approved, you'll receive a QR code confirmation email.

Is the trip suitable for all age groups?+

Designed for all ages. Horton Plains involves moderate walking. Comfortable footwear recommended.

What is the cancellation policy?+

7+ days prior: full refund. Within 7 days: 50% refund. Within 48 hours: no refund.

Who is organizing this trip?+

This expedition is proudly organized by PEAK Adventures — a passionate youth organization dedicated to extraordinary adventures across Sri Lanka.
