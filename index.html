Trip Highlights

01
🏔️
Horton Plains

Stand at the edge of the world. World's End drops 870m into mist-veiled valleys below — a surreal, once-in-a-lifetime encounter with Sri Lanka's wild interior.

02
🌊
Gregory Lake

Glide across the glassy surface of this colonial-era reservoir. Surrounded by manicured gardens and cool mountain air at 1,868m of pure highland serenity.

03
🍃
Tea Estates

Walk through endless emerald terraces. Witness the art of Ceylon tea-making and taste the finest brew at its very source in the world's finest tea country.

04
🌿
Highland Atmosphere

Breathe the freshest air in Sri Lanka at nearly 1,900m. Cool misty mornings, rich greenery, and panoramic highland vistas in every direction.

◈ Information

Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the trip?+

Transport via AC coach, guided tours at all destinations, and entrance fees are included. Meals are at your own expense unless otherwise specified.

What should I wear and bring?+

Warm clothing (Nuwara Eliya mornings can be 10–15°C), comfortable walking shoes, a light rain jacket, sunscreen, and your National ID or passport.

How do I confirm my booking?+

Submit the form below. A PEAK representative will contact you within 24 hours with payment details and your official booking confirmation.

Is the trip suitable for all age groups?+

Designed for all ages. Horton Plains involves moderate walking on uneven terrain — comfortable footwear is recommended. Consult a doctor if you have health concerns.

What is the cancellation and refund policy?+

Cancellations 7+ days prior: full refund. Within 7 days: 50% refund. Within 48 hours: no refund.

Who is organizing this trip?+

This expedition is proudly organized by PEAK — a passionate youth organization dedicated to curating extraordinary adventures across Sri Lanka.
